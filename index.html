About This Project

This atlas visualizes the urban municipalities dataset created by Jessica Jack as part of the Mapping Settler Colonialism in Saskatchewan project at the University of Saskatchewan. The dataset profiles 429 incorporated municipalities from the 1921 federal census, tracking when each community established key colonial institutions.

Events tracked for each settlement include:

  • Founding and incorporation dates
  • Railway arrival and lines served
  • First post office, church, school, cemetery, and newspaper
  • Medical facilities, justice system, residential schools
  • Colonization companies and land offices
